These reports include a wide array of information vital for understanding the data's structure and content. Detailed within these reports are:

  • Dataset Statistics: Summarized statistics that give a snapshot of each dataset's size, number of records, and overall figures related data quality.
  • Value Distributions: Detailed distributions of values within each dataset, highlighting common and rare occurrences.
  • Missing Data Analysis: Identification and quantification of missing values, which is crucial for assessing data completeness and reliability.
  • Memory Usage: Analysis of the memory footprint of each dataset, providing insights into storage and processing requirements.
  • Patient Demographics: Comprehensive breakdowns of patient information, including date of birth and gender.
  • Types of Visits: Classification and frequency of various visit types (e.g., inpatient, outpatient), including their most frequent entry and discharge types.
  • Frequencies of Conditions: Analysis of medical condition prevalence.
  • Frequencies of Procedures: Detailed examination of instances of medical procedures that take place within our facilities.
  • Frequencies of Treatments: Insight into treatments administered by medical personnel within our facilities, highlighting distinct treatments and their overall frequency in medical visits.

These detailed insights are critical for the efficient exploration and analysis of the data, ensuring a thorough understanding before it is transformed into standardized concepts using the OMOP-CDM guidelines.
